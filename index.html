Weekly market summary - Jan Week 03

by Ziye
2024-01-24
225 words - 2 min read
Job related

Fixed income & Currency

China 1 year Medium Term Lending Rate kept unchanged at 2.5%, unexpectedly. Weaker loan demand is observed, so far those stimulus measures from the authorities have not been able to revive credit demand to the extend.

The set of Dec and Q4 activity data in China was published this week. Even though the 2023 and Q4 GDP both came in at 5.2% YoY, which is basically in line with market consensus, the figure itself absorbs the effect of depreciating RMB, the GDP growth doesn’t necessarily mean a 5.2% economic growth.

Such dataset suggests that it’s still weak in the Chinese economy, and more stimulus policies are expected from the government to combat the softening economy.

For UST yields, the higher-than-expected data in retail sales fuelled the rise, affirming the talk of Fed Governor Christopher Waller, who said the US central bank should take a cautious and systematic approach when it begins cutting interest rates, which was further reflected in dollar’s upward trend.

Overall, USD/CNH was in a firm tone last week, trading between the range of 7.1749 - 7.2322.

Meanwhile, some ECB policy makers expressed it’s too early to consider lowering borrowing costs, prompting traders to pull back on bets for rate reductions.

Equity

A+ H shares’ performed weakly after the economy statistics were published, funds flew into Nikkei index ETF.

港岛径第一段行纪-2024年1月21日

by Ziye
2024-01-21
1376 words - 3 min read
Having fun

TLDR版本

路线：以山顶广场为起点，由薄扶林水塘道退出

交通方式：前往山顶广场的交通方式有驾车、山顶缆车和巴士三种，按痛苦程度从低到高排序

长度：8km

用时：步行大概2-2.5h

个人感受：简单路线，人很多，游客尤其多，山径有茂密的林荫，但总体又闷又长

完整版

很难想象在香港呆了一年多的我至今还没有去过西环，这周准备休闲游，于是选定港岛径第一段，顺便感受一下香港大学。

根据导航，我们可以通过出租车、缆车和巴士三种方式前往起点——山顶广场，出租车略贵，缆车已知很拥挤，于是我们决定在湾仔等唯一能够带我们上山的15号巴士。我们在巴士站等了20分钟，看着三辆15号巴士开过而我们却上不了车——因为司机觉得人太多了。讽刺的是，这个15号巴士还自诩为旅游专线巴士，让游客感受到这样的服务态度真的好吗？

等待15号巴士的队列越来越短，大家纷纷寻找其他交通方式，此时第四辆巴士开来，依旧载着满满当当的乘客，我们不抱希望地站在前门，仿佛这样就可以改变什么一样。我原本以为这司机会像前三个司机一样，一脸冷漠地扫我们一眼，然后轻飘飘地把车开走。但不是的，他暴躁地叫车上的乘客腾出位置，又下车来朝我们骂骂咧咧地说着”没位了没位了“，见我们没有走的意思，才重重地叹一口气，放了几个人上车。我们就这样加入了沙丁鱼群大军。

上车后才发现，之后的站点都不会有人上车了，大概是住半山的人不使用巴士这种交通工具，而15路往往在始发站（中环站）就已经基本坐满了。既然需求如此大，为什么不多安排一些巴士来纾解这种压力呢，我不理解。

经历过漫长的山路后，司机终于把我们拉到了山顶广场，这里是一个大型商场，甚至有奈雪的茶，游客特别多。穿过密密麻麻的人群，我们找到了入口。

由路线图和爬升信息可以看到，第一段环山而建，路面平坦且基本没有爬升，是休闲好去处，一路上都看到很多散步、缓跑、遛狗、拍照的行人，非常热闹。卢吉道的山径被修成了类似栈道的形式，可以直接俯瞰到维港和对面九龙及尖沙咀的风景。风景挺好的，就是有点被办公楼影响到心情。

路线本身也是相当护眼的，这里有大片的林荫，可惜今天是阴天天气，如果是夏日艳阳天的话，应该会有让人非常放松的视觉效果。

行至夏力道和克顿道交汇点时，继续延夏力道前行，也可以由克顿道提前退出回到山顶起点处。这之后的路线不像前半段卢吉道那样修缮完善，更多的是土路，同时四周被林荫环绕，视野并不算开阔，总体来说比较沉闷。

到达西高山，这里有一个比较大的瞭望台和凉亭，可以直接看到南丫岛和岛上标志性的发电厂。

由西高山开始，这段行程开始进入后半段。首先是一连串的下坡石阶，踩得人膝盖发酸。此时行人也变少了，大部分都由前边克顿道退出了。这里的景观和港岛径三、四段类似，都是大片的林荫，初见可能很震撼，但是走的时间长了就会有些乏味，甚至有些发昏，因为周围的景观太过类似，无法很清楚地感觉到自己的移动。加上此时天色渐暗，旁边树丛有不知道什么动物在发出窸窸窣窣的声音，让人心里发毛，不知不觉加快脚步。

最后进入薄扶林水塘道，这里又转为大而宽的水泥路，一路向下就可以到薄扶林水塘巴士站离开。对面贝潞道即为第二段的起点，但我们实在提不起兴趣——港岛径好像都挺无聊的。

最后经过了薄扶林水塘，出来到达薄扶林巴水塘士站后，坐车到香港潮商学校站，即可从香港大学站返回香港各区。

香港大学的教学楼，这地方还真是人烟稀少啊？

Weekly market summary - Jan Week 02

by Ziye
2024-01-18
376 words - 2 min read
Job related

Currency

The news that affected the USD/CNH market sentiments was Zhongzhi filed for brankruptcy liquidation, which could affect the subdued onshore sentiments and driving more outflows and in turn supporting USD/CNH. Nevertheless, resistance at 7.19-20 areas was observed.

In the meantime, Chinese authorities indicated that they might lower the amount of money for which banks must set aside as reserves to boost lending, even after the central bank provided a massive amount of liquidity via other tools in recent weeks. PBOC may use open market operations, medium-term lending facilities, and reserve requirements among other monetary policy tools to provide ‘strong’ support for growth in credit. Whatever tools they may use, this policy stance is unlikely to change at least in the near term.

For Yen, it slipped against all G-10 peers as a sharp slowdown in Japanese worker wage growth was seen limiting the BoJ’s ability to exit its ultra-loose policy stance. Yen fell as much as 0.3% to 144.89 per dollar after nominal cash earnings for Japanese workers rose 0.2% in November from the previous year, decelerating sharply from a 1.5% increase in October.

Inflation-adjusted real wages, a key determinant of consumer purchasing power, fell 3.0% in November from a year earlier, faster than a 2.3% decrease in October, data from the labour ministry showed. This has put a question mark on the previous consensus that BoJ is going to dismantle its negative interest rate policy soon.

Fixed income

On 8 Jan, the New York Fed’s over-year consumer inflation survey outlook dropped from 3.4% to 3.0%, the lowest since Jan 2021, which hurt the dollar and UST yields. As of 14 Jan, Investors see a larger likelihood of rate reduction in March and May, but are pricing in a stay at the forthcoming January meeting.

After the New York Fed’s over-year consumer inflation survey outlook turned out to be far below expectation, there is an inflation debate between BlackRock and JPM. For strategists at BR, the monster rally in risk assets of 2023 has room to run well into the new year if inflation continues to ebb. Yet JPM says the optimism around disinflation and subsequent monetary easing is set to be challenged in 2024 as rising geopolitical risks sour sentiment and reignite cost pressures.
